# Code of Conduct / 社区行为准则

We want this project to be a respectful and useful space for builders, researchers, and users working with OCR, Markdown, and RAG.

我们希望这里成为一个面向 OCR、Markdown、RAG 与智能体应用开发者的友善协作空间。

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Explain trade-offs clearly.
- Welcome beginners and domain experts.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private documents or secrets.
- Spam, low-effort promotion, or unrelated content.
- Intentionally misleading benchmark claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that harms the community or exposes private data.
